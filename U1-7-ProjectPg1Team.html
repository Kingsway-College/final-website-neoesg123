Our Team

Aerials Team of 2024-2025: Preview Video

At Kingsway College Aerials, we are a passionate group of individuals who come together to inspire, challenge, and support one another. Our team is made up of dedicated athletes, coaches, and support staff who share a common goal: to draw other nearer to God and bless them through our form of ministry. At Kingsway College Aerials, we believe in fostering a supportive and collaborative environment where every team member feels valued. We embrace diversity, dedication, and teamwork, ensuring that each individual has the opportunity to grow and succeed. Whether on the mat, in the air, or in our team meetings, we constantly strive to be better and help one another achieve greatness.
